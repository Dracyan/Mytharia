An eternal sinner damned by a deal with a devil left to wander the world aimless in what to do with an immortal life, without his wife.

Alberich Ichas, The Eternal Sinner is a controversial figure in Mytharian history. Some see him as a martyr, and others see him as a wicked villain, an eternal sinner. Some argue he has good intentions for what he's done, and others argue he's trying to look like a good person while being a villain in history. Scholars will likely never reach a consensus on this topic.

Personality

Perception of Alberich's personality is divisive and is most commonly looked at in one of two ways.

In one view, Alberich is a tragic martyr, not the type chosen by a god or other being for a tragic death, but just someone who suffers for their cause. Some view him as someone who was once a good person pushed into horrible situations to commit terrible acts to do what he thinks is right. Scholars who believe this view see him as a depressed man who has trouble letting go of the past because it's all he's ever known.

A secondary view, shows Alberich as a monster, a villain. In some circles he is seen as a man who believes he's a good person but wants to put up a facade of thinking he is a bad person when in reality he is one. This view states that he is a man obsessed with the past incapable of moving on, someone who refuses to change even with a thousand years of opportunities, and a thousand years of blood on his hands. This view states that he is a man obsessed with legacy who wants to be seen as a tragic hero, a tragic martyr, someone who would go so far as to kill witnesses that would tell stories different from what he wants.

Backstory

Early History

Alberich was born to a military house in Resanthum, House Ichas. He grew up training to join the military as a medic and eventually did when he came of age to because his father, Vishnyakov Ichas, was a renowned general in Resanthum's military. Eventually when he was in the woods he met a woman named Neveah Ravana. She was a member of a powerful witches coven hidden in the mountains of Resanthum. The two eventually became close, despite the danger of Alberich colluding with a witch due to the laws of Resanthum that all witches be burned if their magic doesn't come from The Golden Phoenix. His family was not happy with this development but Alberich continued to see her and eventually was disowned by his family for it. The two eventually married when Alberich was 26.


Nuka Cola, "Human Alberich", 2024

Alberich and Neveah would go on to sire a child together, but due to the danger of Neveah being a witch within her coven in a country that burns those born lucent, they chose to give up the child for adoption. The two later would attempt to have another child but it would die shortly after birth.

On Alberich's 35th birthday in 842 FA, after not hearing from his father for nearly 10 years, Vishnyakov came to visit him. Surprisingly, it wasn't to fight him, but as a cry for help. He had become incredibly sick and was asking if Neveah had any dark magic which could heal him. Alberich protested, he didn't want to help his father, but Neveah convinced him that they should help. She had been studying ancient magic and had spent years shaping them into a dark spell, something that seemed to have the power to grant strength, to heal, perchance to bring back the dead. It had seemed to emerge from the shadows to her, and she used it to save Alberich's father from the sickness that plagued him. This spell would later become known as Devoid of Life.

Devoid of Life, was not a spell without consequence, it was a spell that fed on the soul for the power it granted. Whether it be the soul of the caster, or other souls with which they had access too. It damaged her soul which in turn took its toll on her body. She didn't realise the price at first and tried to hide it from Alberich because she didn't want him to hurt himself. But 6 years later she began the crawl into death. She became bedridden for the last of her days, and Alberich as an experienced doctor couldn't figure out what was plaguing his wife or how to save her. And eventually she died. She left behind her spellbook with the secrets of Devoid of Life. Her death destroyed him mentally and physically. He tried to spend the next 2 years finding peace, coming to terms with her death. But in all that time, he just couldn't. In Resanthum, life is not meant to be fleeting, if you live a good life in the eyes of The Golden Phoenix it's said that they shall resurrect you and allow you to live forever. Love should not be confined to the bounds of a single lifetime, and all should be granted the gift of resurrection.

Becoming The Eternal Sinner

Over the course of the following year he studied every page of Neveah's spellbooks and journals to try and find any way he could to bring her back. He eventually came across mentions of a terribly powerful devil, the archdevil of souls and one of the most powerful devils to ever live called Kowathlich. Alberich pled to Kowathlich to resurrect his wife, and the two struck a deal. Kowathlich would resurrect Neveah, and in return, after 1 year together Kowathlich would come and claim Alberich's soul. Should Alberich honour the deal, Neveah will be left alive.

The year is bittersweet. The two enjoyed their time together as best they could, becoming inseparable during their one year together. Neveah often made her banana bread for Alberich, his favourite dessert, knowing their time would be short as gloom hangs over them, the gloom of Kowathlich looming in the distance and getting closer each day. And as the year ended, in 853 FA Kowathlich came to claim Alberich's soul. He beckoned Al to make his final goodbyes but Alberich defied him and said he won't be giving up his soul. Kowathlich was humoured that a mortal thought he could escape his contract that easily, especially when he tried to raise a sword, a toothpick towards him. They fought but when Kowathlich grew tired of this charade he ignited Neveah in flames. She burned to death screaming in pain as Alberich watched in horror. For attempting to defy the deal, Kowathlich decided not just to take his soul, but to transform him into a tiefling of his own design bearing close resemblance to Kowathlich's own form.

Nuka Cola, "Alberich Split", 2024

Alberich sat there, left to rot next to the charred remains of his wife, his world. Not knowing what else he could even do, he wandered off in a fugue state, off to explore his prison or what others would it the world.

Skyfrost

518 years later in 284 ME he found the city of Skyfrost and stayed there for a few months as his presence became known within the town. Skyfrost didn't enjoy magic users nor did it enjoy Tieflings. Alberich was about to be chased from the city when a burst of arcane energy hit the town shaking it with destructive consequence, as Kowathlch clawed his way back up from the gloom world with a small armada of devils by his side. He sought to plunge the city into the gloom and claim the souls of all those residing within so that he could become even more powerful and further his own goals of amassing more and more souls and power.

Alberich did not want the city to be destroyed, this city he fell in love with, and so he decided to fight Kowathlich himself but was defeated and only barely managed to escape without being killed. Kowathlich tightened his grip on the city and began to claim the souls of those within. So Alberich set off to find ways of getting stronger so he could singlehandedly defeat Kowathlich.

He traveled 6 months on foot back to Resanthum and back to Neveah's grave to try and contact her spirit again. He had tried it thousands of times before but this time, it worked and she appeared before him. She told him the truth about Devoid of Life and how it could be used to channel souls to gain power, similar to Kowathlich's own power as the archdevil of souls.

Once again, Alberich set off for 6 months on foot back towards Skyfrost. He made his return secretly and went through the city and found every citizen promising them salvation, he got them to lend him their power, their souls so that he could have the power to fight Kowathlich. When he felt enough of the strength flowing through him, he went to Kowathlich at the heart of the city and challenged him to a duel again. Kowathlich laughed but accepted and the two battled. They did not sleep, they only fought for the next 2 weeks with no rest. Even with all this power Alberich was still losing, Kowathlich was still more powerful than Alberich powered by a city of souls. So Alberich channeled all the souls he had claimed as all the citizens of Skyfrost died one by one so he could strike down Kowathlich, but even then he didn't have the strength. So he made a binding vow, a forbidden art of magic even in circles that practice magic more liberally. He used the power of the souls and sealed Kowathlich within his own body, within his own mind. He burned all the souls inside his body, even the last embers of his own that Kowathlich had left him with him so he wouldn't be too suicidal, and Kowathlch became the only soul, the only spirit in his body. As Kowathlich became bound within Alberich's body, he was impressed with the cunning to be able to defeat him, to subjugate him in this way as the two viciously made out within the mindscape.

With Kowathlch sealed in his own mind and nearly the entire population of Skyfrost killed, Alberich walked through the ghost town. There were few survivors of the carnage, but Alberich found a girl not more than 6 years old crying next to her dead family because Alberich had borrowed then spent their souls earlier. He looked at her as she cried and cursed at him and he killed her. Then he left the dead city.

He returned to Neveah's spirit and she said that it was time for her to rest, and depart for the afterlife as she was a spirit without worship, and she had persisted for too long.

Alberich reflected in this moment, seeing himself not the same as he once was. He had changed, he had become The Eternal Sinner bound to walk Mytharia forever more. Neveah said that she was only the reflection of his past now and he can't let go but he has to. The rain poured down on them and they sat together and he cried as he knew he was the only one who could keep her memory alive. He would continue to chase his dream of being with her, but he knew that it was nigh impossible. She won't come back to life, and he cannot pass into the afterlife without a soul.

Alberich stared at his own reflection in the water, disgusted by his own visage and he danced with the spirit of his wife Neveah as she disappeared and faded into the afterlife.

So Alberich The Eternal Sinner swore that he would one day reunite with her, no matter what it takes.

Impact

In the wake of Skyfrost, racism against tieflings becomes far worse because of the death caused by the tiefling Alberich and the devils he fought. So much death as he wandered the world, just adding to the stereotype of devils and tieflings being murderers and stealers of souls for their own gain. Many communities in the outer reaches of Faiftexl began to burn tiefling children again after a brief recession at the beginning of the Age of Memories with the higher rate of tiefling births and the global mass deaths.

Modern History

During the age of memories, in 1017 ME, Alberich wandered to and lived in Greenest City for a time. on 25/11/1017 ME, Alberich met The Outsiders, a recently formed group of adventurers who at the time were searching for a way to cure the cancer of a member of their party. Alberich ended up going shopping with them in town and becoming acquainted with them.

Alberich was watching the Greenest Day Parade which celebrated the founding of Greenest and all its various exports when the 25/11/1017 ME terrorist attack happened. Alberich was among the adventurers and guards who fought against the cultists who sought to cause chaos for reasons that remain unknown as all the cultists who were caught killed themselves. Once the battle was over he threw his axe right next to DOOR's head with a message before leaving for the night.

The next day, Alberich was then present when DOOR and Spider-Volt fought in the streets of the city, and further when the original Spider-Volt confronted his imposter for all the murders he had committed back in Rextaulia, stating that he planned to take the imposter to prison. He became more occupied however when he saw and recognized Alberich standing just a few feet away from him.

The original Spider-Volt was taken aback to see such a prolific criminal and one who was responsible for the deaths of thousands of innocent people over the last thousand years. He was forced to shift his focus towards the bigger threat, towards Alberich instead and demanded Alberich come along quietly to prison. Alberich refused, attempting to say that he had been to prison many times but they always rotted around him and that he would outlast any prison he was put into. As the original Spider-Volt argued with him, Alberich repeated his talking points several times, and went on to say that he was cursed and that was the reason for those deaths, and that part of the curse was his disgusting appearance. The assertation that his appearance of being a tiefling, was disgusting, angered Spider-Volt deeply as he pointed to DOOR and asked Alberich if he was saying that thing child was a disgusting monster for being born a tiefling. Alberich did not respond. As Spider-Volt was getting ready to take away two of The Outsiders party members, they acted quickly and cast a suggestion onto Spider-Volt that he leave. The gambit bought them the time they needed as The Outsiders, joined now by Alberich, absconded into the forests outside the city to head into a remnant ruin that hadn't been disturbed since The Saviours of Greenest were there before The Planaclysm.

Alberich remained outside with Raziel and the newborn Fence while The Outsiders raided the dungeon. When they returned after the delve, DOOR decided he wanted to fuck with Alberich. So after hearing many many stories of Alberich and his deceased wife, Neveah, DOOR decided to conjure an illusion of Alberich's wife returned from the dead and having made him a fresh pan of banana bread so excited to see him and be with him again. Alberich was destroyed, he was excited but also he couldn't believe what he was seeing, he never thought he would see her again as he cried at her return. Then DOOR dropped the illusion, and showed that it was all a lie saying that his wife was dead and she was never coming back.

This is known as The Banana Bread Incident. In this moment Alberich was so wracked with emotions and his mental state was completely and utterly obliterated. In this moment Kowathlich found the opportunity to take control and have some fun. He partially manifested through Alberich's body, the snapped off horns grew to full size, Alberich himself grew in height and the eyes glowed with a sinister energy when Kowathlich took the reigns of the body. Kowathlich toyed with them, he didn't bring out his famous scythe of souls, nor did he open his sanctum of will. Even the great archangel of gambling Raziel was but a pawn in his game. The Outsiders were however, a bit more impressive than Kowathlich imagined. He was just about to get serious and attempt to manifest more of his power through his sanctum of will so he could claim all their souls when Cresie Beilar ran at him with her halbert burning bright with all the radiant energy she could muster, having prayed to all the luffistic deities to grant her strength in this moment. Cresie stabbed Kowathlich aiming for the heart as the adamantine plate shattered under the weight of the divine and ricocheted her halbert through Alberich's arm but still burning his chest with the proximity as she sent Kowathlich back into Alberich's mind.

The Outsiders stood barely standing and covered horrified in their blood from the battle. Alberich laid on the ground his arm separate as Cal MeowMeow broke off one of his horns while his body was still weak. Alberich passed out in a pool of clear blood as The Outsiders realised Kowathlich could have killed them if he wasn't playing with them. They couldn't deal with him if that happened again. They casted sending on Spider-Volt and told him where they were and to come pick up Alberich. When he arrived he saw them all still covered in blood while the party hid the imposter, saying that he had fried himself to death because he wasn't immune to his own lightning. Spider-Volt thanked them for calling him and said he was glad they were ok and that he was impressed that they managed to survive kowathlich. He collected Alberich who swore that the prison would rot around him and he would one day be wandering the world again, and Spider-Volt swung away after signing DOOR's door.

When Spider-Volt returned to Renexann, he placed Alberich in his hidden prison where all his villains are kept at the highest security. He placed Alberich in The Archivist's Cube where he couldn't harm anyone else for as long as he remained inside.

Goals

Alberich's only definitively known goal is to find a way to reunite with his wife Neveah, whether that be for her to be resurrected and live again, or for him to find a way to regain his soul and pass into the afterlife and see her again, whether she's in The Gloom World, or The Bright World. Attaining a new soul however is nigh impossible without the intervention of gods since his was destroyed after the events in Skyfrost. Resurrection of Neveah is also impossible as history states that her soul doesn't wish to return to life, she wanted him to move on from her when her soul passed into the afterlife and for her to return would keep Alberich stuck in the past.

Relationships

Neveah Ichas

Neveah Ichas was Alberich's wife before her death due to her use of Devoid of Life. The two had a very loving relationship in life but parts of it were marred in gloom, especially during her year of return. Alberich loves his wife, perhaps too much. He's hung up on her even a thousand years after her death. However, this is in line with the culture Alberich and Neveah grew up in and lived in for most of their mortal lives. They grew up in Resanthum, the country of resurrection, and of immortal love. Resanthian culture believes that people should always return to life in some form or another, and that true love transcends lifetimes. Neveah requested Alberich move on from her and to move on from the past, but he has denied that wish in favour of trying to find a way to return to her.

Kowathlich

Kowathlich and Alberich have a complicated relationship that in some circles would be described as a kismesissitude. Alberich holds deep hatred for Kowathlich for taking his soul and for killing Neveah. But he's also deeply attracted to him because of the depths of this hatred. Kowathlich is entertained by Alberich and his boldness to defy and even fight him time and time again, and then of course to subjugate him inside his own body. Because of this their unholy attraction to one another is mutual, but through hatred rather than any form of love or affection. Over the last 800 years they've been bound together, the two have grown close but the disdain has remained even so while an amount of care has blossomed because Alberich would die to animaleipis if Kowathlich were to be destroyed or leave his body. There have even been cases where they worked together toward common goals and then consummated their hatred within the mindscape.

Vishnyakov Ichas

Alberich has a complex relationship with his father. There was disdain between them for many years due to Alberich's love for his witch wife, then things seemed to be better when Neveah healed him, but it didn't really bring them back together. They didn't see each other many times before his death in 907 FA, and Alberich visited him one final time on his death bed.

The Outsiders

The Outsiders and Alberich only met for a brief time, but from that brief time The Outsiders hold great contempt for Alberich. They see him as a man stuck in the past, and an evil villain who thinks he's the tragic martyr of the story. Cal wishes that Alberich could be free of Kowathlich, move on and live a normal life away from his evil wife, but Cal also wants to kill him. Cresie and DOOR also want the old man dead and Cresie regrets not dealing a finishing blow when she had the chance, even if he would have reformed a few years later, but not accepting that he would.

Equipment

Adamantine Armour

From his travels and his time in Rascatine, Alberich managed to obtain a full suit of pure adamantine plate armour that would fetch hundreds of thousands of gold with the right wealthy buyers. This armour is near impervious to harm and can absorb particularly fierce blows into becoming like any other strike.
