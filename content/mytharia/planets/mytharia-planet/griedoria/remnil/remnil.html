Remnil is one of two dominant countries in Griedoria. It was established shortly after The Planaclysm by refugees from the fallen countries of Dougleton, Arcadium, and Dawnforge. The noble houses that once governed Dougleton quickly became dominant again as the country grew its foothold with the survivors of The Planaclysm. The country flourished mainly in the fields of arcane research like Arcadium and Dougleton had done before, but also took an interest in the documentation of history should another disaster like The Planaclysm occur again in future.

In the current age, Remnil is home to some of the brightest and most powerful mages in Mytharia who work to further arcane research. One of the primary places of study being REMU, a university and place of study for those gifted with magic.

Geography

The country is primarily wooded with a wet climate due to its close proximity to the ocean. A mountain range leftover from when Griedoria and Vortil split during The Planaclysm decorates the northwestern region of Remnil just south of the city of Dungeon.

Society

Remnil's society is very independant with a focus on self-sufficence. There is a lot of community around research and study with it being very easy usually to find research partners when drafting experiments and the like. However, outside of the realm of magic and study, the people of Remnil generally believe that it's your job to find your place in society and support yourself and your family.

Remnil's main form of worship and religion, is in the worship of the departed spirits of their ancestors. Citizens of Remnil usually keep detailed family histories and family trees, and will keep shrines to their most venerated ancestors to attempt to call on their spirits for guidance when needed. This is especially common among the noble houses of Remnil, and among those with spellcasters in their family.

The most common careers in Remnil are those in the spellcasting class of society; Enchanters, Summoners, Spirit Binders, and the like. Adventurers are also very common especially in Remnil's second largest city, Dungeon because the city is half-built into a dungeon that seems to be constantly shifting and growing beneath the city.

Within the country, necromancy is the only illegal form of magic. Similarly, most undead are killed on sight by the country's military. Skeletons, vampires, and zombies being among those undead that are executed swiftly.

Government

A web of interconnected noble houses govern Remnil, lead by House Starsteel who governed Dougleton before its destruction. Among these houses are House Adamantine which controls the largest amount of military within the country, House Orichalcum which holds control over the healing guilds in the country, and House Stygite which has the largest hold over commerce and trade, being the house that mints the currency.

Another noble house, House Mythralight was also considered one of the great noble houses of Remnil until its recent destruction. Its unknown if any survivors remain, other than the Mythralight's who broke off just before The Planaclysm and are believed to still wander the world. House Mythralight previously held control over the mages guild of Remnil, The Lustrous Mist. But since then House Starsteel has taken control.

The government is organised with House Starsteel, and King Rozam Starsteel in the central administration and governance of the country, while delegating tasks and aspects of leadership and administration to the other great noble houses and representatives

Trade

Though close geographically to Kindwyn due to being neighbours on the continent, Remnil and Kindwyn don't share a very close relationship or trade very much, but they do share a small amount of trade with food. Kindwyn also exports some magical plants that only grow there to Remnil.

Remnil's biggest trade partners are with Resanthum, and Rextaulia due to their large exports of gems and metals respectively.

History

The country was founded in 5 ME, by King Robril Starsteel shortly after the end of The Planaclysm. He lead a large group of refugees from Arcadium, Dougleton, and Dawnforge across Vortil eventually making it to where New Dougleton stands now before Griedoria split off from Vortil.

New Dougleton grew quickly due to the amount of mages among the settlers, and from the dawnian dwarves' skills in construction. The country later expanded north and surveyors discovered a massive dungeon at the northwestern edge of the continent in 22 ME. Eventually, the civilisation within the dungeon was peacefully integrated (wow how often does that happen) into Remnil as a whole. This city became known as Dungeon.

Rumours and Legends

There are some rumours of spirits going missing around the country, especially in the capitol. some believe there's a secret project being worked on to try and innoculate the population with abilities from these kidnapped spirits.

Some believe that the fall of House Mythralight was not an unfortunate accident, but something orchestrated by higher powers in the country.

Notable Locations

New Dougleton

The capitol city of the country, New Dougleton is home to a lot of the magical research in the country, as well as the castle of House Starsteel and King Rozam Starsteel. New Dougleton is known as a good place to find magical materials and supplies, and contains a grand library of all historic events and books the scribes of Remnil have managed to assemble. Some say this library even contains a few records from the Age of Lost.

Dungeon

Considered by some to be sort of the shadow capitol, Dungeon is the largest city in Remnil. It extends not only on the surface, but into the depths of an evershifting dungeon that seems to continually renew the magic artifacts, materials, and monsters housed within. Dungeon is also considered the financial capitol of the country due to the heavy exporting of items and materials from within. It is however also the city with the most deaths annually due to the consistent stream of not only crown hired adventurers, but visiting adventurers coming to delve into the dungeon at the city's heart.

Remnil University

This university is considered by most to be the best theoretical and practical magic university in Mytharia. It has incredible facilities and resources, alongside its staff and centuries of research on magic. REMU itself is also a university town with several different campus buildings with different specialisations in the town, and staff and students typically living on-site. Most would-be mages aspire to go to REMU if possible, especially over options like Sir Antoine A. Adventure's Adventuring Academy.

Inhabitants

Most commonly found within Remnil are humans, pimawtawn, and dwarves because of the countries original settlers. Minotaurs later became a large population in the country after the integration of Dungeon. Its elven population is comprised of drow who were shunted to Remnil from The Feywild during The Planaclysm. The country also has an average spirit population and a relatively high population of birdfolk due to their arrival from The Skylands.
