Malphas' are a form of devil born from the corruption of different bird spirits such as harpies. They represent the concept of The Tower, constructing fortresses and knocking down those of others. They are birds made of shadows known to be able to meld into them and form grand creations from within the darkness around them.

### Appearance
They take the form of bipedal birds with arms and wings and multiple feathered tails. their wings are usually partially skeletal as black feathers extend off them. They have slender beaks filled with teeth. They possess bird legs with sharp talons and dexterous hands on slender arms
