Malevolent spirits are spirits corrupted away from the concept they initially represented and turned toward violence and torture in the pursuit of power. Malevolent spirits can take many forms but are by in large divided into two categories, Devils, and Demons. Devils are malevolent spirits that possess sapience, while Demons act purely on the emotion of violence and their prime concept.

### Appearance
Malevolent Spirits possess a wide array of appearances but are prone to jagged tusks or teeth and claws, dark and desaturated colours. They typically look like gloom world corrupted variants of other spirits.

### Genetics and Reproduction
Some Devils and Demons retain the ability to reproduce through means similar to their original forms. breaking off pieces of their essence to manifest smaller spirits, or even reproducing sexually. Tieflings are the result of many devils and demons parlaying with mortals.

### Theorised Origin
It is theorised the first malevolent spirit rose during the age of dawn when some spirits first began to deny their prime concepts and orders from the gods that created them. These spirits were cast down and began the first devils and demons.
