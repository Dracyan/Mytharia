### Theorised Origin


Ruby Trolls
(Cresie)
Sometimes born with the ability to contact the dead or with some psionic telekinetic abilities. Usually live to around 50

Topaz Trolls
(Tavros)
Sometimes born with the ability to speak with animals or spirits. Usually live to around 60

Lemon Trolls
(solluxander)
Often born with powerful psionics, often moreso than ruby trolls but can sometimes be sensitive to psychic effects that may overload them. sometimes born with laser vision. Similar lifespans to humans.

Bloodstone Trolls
(based a little on karkat)
Usually possess regenerative blood, similar to vampires. usually live around 100 years.

Cat's Eye Trolls
(Nepeta)
Often born with sharp claws and some feline traits, such as ears. usually live around 125 years

Jade Trolls
(kanaya)
Often born with sharp teeth, often prey upon bloodstone trolls for their regenerative blood. They drink blood. Usually live around 160 years

Apatite Trolls
(terezi)
Often born with psionics relating to divination and resisting the influence and controls of others. Commonly have powerful minds. Usually live to around 200

Flourite Trolls
(Briska, Spider-Volt's Wife)
Often born with powerful psionics for controlling peoples minds. Born from spider-like grubmothers. Usually live to around 250

Lapis Trolls
(Equius)
strong. often build and enchant items. Usually live to around 400

Garnet Trolls
(Gamzee)
Very strong and resistant to heat. Often more durable than others and grow much larger. Have huge lung capacities and huge pressure resistances including to the ocean and to space. Usually live to around 500

Amethyst Trolls
(eridan)
Amethyst Trolls are amphibious, able to live in water and on land. They often possess more fishlike qualities, such as tails, and always have gills and fins. These trolls are usually born with the ability to smell blood. Usually live to around 700

Rubellite Trolls
(fefari)
Amphibious, strong, nearly always born with incredible psionics. Often have poisonous skin or blood. Usually live to around 1000

Diamond Trolls 
The rarest type of troll, troll religion believes the afterlife was created by a diamond troll. Incredibly fast and strong with powerful psionics and amphibiousness, as well as their own regenerative blood and some camouflaging abilities. Lifespan unknown.
