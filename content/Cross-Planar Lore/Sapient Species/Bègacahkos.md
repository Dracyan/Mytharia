The bègacahkos are a race of humanoids resembling Mytharian hippopotamus'. Not much is known about their race due to the small number of them currently known in Mytharian record.

### Appearance
Bègacahkos are a bipedal race resembling hippopotamus'. They most often have short limbs but large bodies with lots of mass which to outside viewers may resemble fat, but is actually pure muscle. Covering their bodies is a thin sheet of hair that grows longer on the head around the top of the skull and at the front of the snout, resembling human head and facial hair. Bègacahkos skin is typically a shade of grey or brown and is rough and flabby. This skin also secretes a form of red gel which protects them from solar radiation.
 
Focusing more on the head, bègacahkos have small nubby ears that often perk up when excited or droop down when stressed or sad. They have wide snouts with large canine and incisor teeth in their mouths that can be exceedingly deadly with their powerful jaws.

### Genetics and Reproduction
Bègacahkos reproduce through standard sexual means, however they possess a strange dichotomy of form. In most cases they appear in their standard form, and while in this form they are consistently and constantly horny. But when exposed to the pheromones of specific plants on the surface of their homeworld, their horizontal mass grows to become vertical with them standing taller and appearing more lean. They also grow several spike-like protrusions out of their spine. While affected by these hormones they lose all libido and become more hyper-focused on scholarly matters.

Bègacahkos also have a strange reaction to moonlight as it seems to charge their cells with energy, stopping the decay of sells and speeding up the generation of new cells. This grants them incredible strength, as well as immortality.

### History
The bègacahkos are a race from a far off planet that has been filled with civil war and classism. The society of the bègacahkos was divided into the wealthy elites living on the surface of the planet in lush gardens and bathing in the moonlight, with the poor living below the surface, unable to access the power granted by the light of the moon. During the planet's first civil war, they destroyed their moon and thus the power of the moon was lost as the immortals of the upper echelons were shunted back down to mortality. Overtime, the moon's power became but a legend and a myth for generations who had not experienced it to doubt if it were ever real.

With the thing creating the class divide destroyed, the disparity lessened as most bègacahkos returned to the surface. At this time most took on their alpha forms but during the harsh winters would retreat into the mountains or back underground where the pheromones which caused that form would not affect them, causing most reproduction to happen in the winter months.

The bègacahkos eventually became involved in an interstellar war with the [[vulpalf]]. The vulpalf were brutal with their advanced technology. Some sects of the bègacahkos aligned themselves with the vulpalf and eventually the bègacahkos homeworld was destroyed with very few survivors managing to escape across the stars in ships which would eventually run out of life support. Those who had flipped to the side of the vulpalf were exterminated shortly after the war. The current number of living bègacahkos is unknown.

### Cultural Ideals

#### Naming Traditions
Bègacahkos names are typically focused on the ideals of battles and warriors. They name their children things like Nialls, meaning warrior, Warren, Koa, and Vanqui.
