### Appearance
Saiyans are a race that look similar to humans with typically muscular bodies but are prone to hairy bodies, especially on the forearms and legs. They are born with prehensile tails but sometimes have them cut off for some reason or another.
They don't have a fully set average height within the range and tend toward extreme variety in height. some saiyans also go beyond the range being far taller, particularly titan-born saiyans
