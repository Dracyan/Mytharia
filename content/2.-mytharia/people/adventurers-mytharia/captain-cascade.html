Captain Cascade was a Water Genasi human from Lazulain. They grew up in the lower class of this country before being chosen by Mako Levith as his spouse. During their marriage Mako turned Cascade into a vampire. While in the noble class of Lazulain they studied magic intensely and became a powerful mage, later killing their husband and leaving the country to become an infamous pirate captain.

Cascade would go on to have their crew and ship destroyed by The Chaos Crew in Kamamel 1018 ME leading to them joining the crew of The Abyssal Voyager. The captain was killed during the crews quest for The Fang of the Abyss when they fought a beholder that supressed their vampirism and disintegrated them.

Personality

Some described them as being as bold as the progenitor of their sireline, Forsythia Mythralight. Cascade being unafraid to speak their mind and strike out at others in the pursuit of power and acclaim. They were mischevious but helpful to their friend, providing their aid to allies they believed to be worthy.
The captain was also known for their romantic and sexual dalliances with other pirates, often originating from a place of flirting with enemies on the battlefield until it developed into something more on the enemies defeat, escape, or eventual rematch.

Background

Early History

Cascade was born in a small town outside the capitol of Lazulain to human and kryfora parents. The family were part of Lazulain's lower class. Due to Cascade being born lucent, their parents got them as much of a magical education as they could afford. Cascade grew to be a decently powerful mage and in their young adulthood caught the notice of Mako Levith who took Cascade as his spouse.

Being elevated to the noble class, Cascade gained more resources to study the arcane with the main caveat being that they were fed on occasionally by their vampiric husband. This feeding was eventually too much and Mako accidentally killed and turned Cascade with his over-excited feeding. Upon their turn Cascade had to relearn all the magic they'd spent their life learning. Eventually some time after their turn, fearing for their fate with the inability to be a consistent food source any longer, Cascade killed their own husband and fled the country seeking to become an outlaw and a pirate.

Pirate Days

During their days as a pirate, Cascade eventually managed to procure a vessel their own and become a captain. They hired several crew members and became a patron of magic to those they trusted. During the Storms of Ruin in 1018 ME, Captain Cascade fought the crew of the abyssal voyager in an attempt to steal what they knew about the fang. During the battle they ended up flirting with Caspian during a swordfight while the chaos crew killed off the rest of their crew (aside from Aaarrrgghh) and sunk Cascade's ship. Being defeated, Cascade sought to at least preserve their own life and offered to join their crew. Going down with their ship wouldn't have done much since they were a denizen of the sea and could breathe underwater unlike their ship.

The Chaos Crew joined by Cascade made their way to Myscelin, a small island that was once a sanctuary for worshippers of The Mistress of the Abyss. The crew found early into their venture that the island was infested with Mycelian Zombies, Cascade luckily couldn't be infected due to their vampiric nature. The crew found a map to the fang's resting place after an unfortunate encounter between Harry Gucc and a zombie beholder, then made heading towards Juno to stock up on supplies before the last leg of the quest.

While in Juno, Cascade looked into procuring a new ship, before Cas of The Chaos Crew asked them to accompany the crew in a hunt for a Beholder. This quest would prove fatal as when the party arrived in the lair of the beholder its antimagic vision supressed Cascade's vampirism causing them to fall to the ground due to the injuries that first caused their death and turn into a vampire. The party fought and tried to protect their friend, but as the battle drew near to its close, the bolder sent off a ray of disintegration reducing the captain to dust. Their death had a great impact on Caspian as in rage and grief he finally tapped into his angelic heritage and summoned radiant energy to dispatch the beholder. But the beholders death was too late to prevent Cascade's.

Relationships

Mako Levith

The two had a complicated relationship. Their marriage was one created out of Mako's search for entertainment and consistent sustenance. To Cascade, he was a danger, but did put them on a path and life they otherwise never would have had. As Mako's spouse, Cascade gained the ability to study magic much more in-depthely then they could have otherwise, and their union lead to Cascade's turn to vampirism. Their relationship was not a happy marriage, but it defined the trajectory of both their lives.

Caspian

In their short time knowing one another, the two shared a consistent flirtation, but Cascade's death arrived too quickly for the two to discuss what their relationship was, or was not.
