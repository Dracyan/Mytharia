Florian is a former soldier of the greenest empire, turned spy for grognak who was later executed for his crimes before rising as a Martyr of Karamy. Upon rising he aided in the attempt to uncover the conspiracy surrounding the missing Millicent of Frostont and other spirits who had been disappearing from the streets of New Dougleton. Upon forming The Chaos Crew with his allies at the end of this quest, they ended up taking to the seas so they could search for The Fang of The Abyss in hopes to prevent the aquatic calamity set to befall The Divergent Isles.

Personality

Florian is a pessimistic leaning soldier. He says what he thinks when he can allow himself too without speaking in riddles, but he's still more guarded on his past and the things that he regrets in life. He has some anger issues, but the anger is most often internalised and directed at himself. He's selfless, but because he doesn't care about what happens to him thinking that anything which befalls him is just what he deserves for the things that he has done.

Backstory

Florian is a former soldier of The Greenest Empire.
Even before the war he came from a patriotic family with his parents looking fondly toward the military of Greenest and encouraging him to enlist once the drafting started before the war with Grognak. His siblings, Soren, and Seraphina, sought to avoid their parents and their patriotism, but while Florian was still there he still believed.

Once the drafts began and Florian was drafted into the military, during training he met Renata as the two clicked and became fast friends. However, they didn't discuss the politics of the war nor the grim events which surrounded their lives. Florian also continued to talk with his sister even once the war began. She could tell he was deeply brainwashed and she slowly tried to talk him out of it without outing herself as being against the empire.

In the battlegrounds, Florian was placed as a scout to watch for enemies or to check out the surrounding areas for strategic positioning. Him and Renata didn't see each other again after training, seemingly never in the same squads or parties. until one day Florian was on the battlefield fighting against a party of Grognak soldiers. He fought and as he struck down a Grognak solider he didn't see who it really was, that it was Renata, until the blow had been struck and she was on the ground horribly injurred. Paralysed in the wake of his actions, with the battle not going well, and his party losing to those from the Grognak party, Florian felt like he could die now and that it would be what he deserved. He stood there, blank, his party had to drag him from the battlefield in retreat so that he would not be killed.

Upon his later death at war, the God Karamy, The False Sufferer was impressed by his suffering and chose to raise his soul as a spirit and place it into the greatsword he once wielded so he might rise and become a great martyr to fated death more spectacular and tragic than the one that had already befallen him.

His ascent from the sword however, was put on hold, as the spirit binder, Jean-Guy of Wingspan was travelling to Greenest City and happened to hear about the anomaly of the sword that had changed. And so, he purchased it from the military and brought it back to New Dougleton for study among the many other artifacts and bound spirits which he kept.

Then from there, eventually when the group who would become known as The Chaos Crew was raiding Jean Guy's office, Florian's vampiric sword fell to the ground and it jostled him awake and out of the sword. He was now, Karamy's martyr, and it was time for him to fight again so it seemed.

Relationships

Seraphina

During the early days of the war, Seraphina, Florian's older sister kept in contact with him through the patriotism and she still tried to talk with him seeing how brainwashed he was to the cause. She slowly tried to get him out of that mess as she dodged the draft to war herself, and while trying to avoid getting outed for her beliefs against the empire.
