The 'cock and ball torture wizard' is a strange figure known vaguely across Mytharia in arcane social circles. He's an immortal man who's dedicated his life to researching cock and ball torture, and all the ways it can be achieved consensually. Some say he's one of the most powerful mages alive, despite his strange specialisation.

Personality

A strange man, The cock and ball torture wizard is known for his eccentric personality, especially when it comes to cock and ball torture. He's known to be a very generous man upon meeting him, willing to help any wandering travelers he comes across. He is however a staunch pacifist and refuses to harm anyone in any way that isn't consensual.

Background

What is known about The Cock and Ball Torture Wizard's background is shrouded in rumour and heresay. Various accounts state he claims to have come from the south pole, but many historians dismiss that as impossible.

Some rumours say the source of his immortality was a pact forgoing his genitals for everlasting life. These rumours go further to state that he studies cock and ball torture to provide others with a pleasure he can never feel.


For a brief period of time in 1,008 ME, The Cock and Ball Torture accompanied The Crunch Crack Pirate Crew on some quests, but later left the crew due to their violent nature.

Relationships

Alberich Ichas

At some point between the fall of Skyfrost in 285 ME, and the fall of The Calamity Herald in 462 ME, The Cock and Ball Torture Wizard had a torrid and toxic affair with Alberich Ichas. The two were lovers for some time, but their relationship was deeply toxic leaving both with only sour memories of the ordeal after their breakup.

The Joker

The Joker and The Cock and Ball Torture Wizard have a strange relationship. Many consider The Joker to be the dark mirror to The Cock and Ball Torture Wizard, being an entity of pure chaos, hijinx, and destruction, and having originated from The North Pole.

Some cults have arisen across Mytharia worshipping the two as the enders of days, believing that someday when they meet, they will either battle, or have intense sex that brings about the destruction of the world as it is currently known.
