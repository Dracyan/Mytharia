Forythia Mythralight is an ancient vampiric wizard and the progenitor of all vampires. She has been sighted many times since she left wider society after her turn into a Vampire, but her goals and whereabouts since The Planaclysm and the fall of Dougleton.

What is known of her current day is that she travels with her husband Rhodri Mythralight but not with her children.

Before she became a vampire, she was the head of house Mythralightfrom 1,052 FA to 2 ME. She rebuilt it from the brink of destruction in the wake of her fathers death and became the arcane advisor to the at the time king Robril Starsteel before she left it behind to found Greenest City. Her leaving that position for such a strange reason was shocking at the time, but it became clear later that she had done so for the express purpose of the ritual which transformed her and her family into the first vampires.

Personality

Forsythia is a haughty noble. Though she is not truly part of a noble house anymore, she still acts like the peak of nobility which is likely due in part to her arrogance at knowing that she is one of the most powerful beings in Mytharia and there is nary a chance that anyone should have the ability to kill her.

Forsythia is an embodiment of arrogance and belief that she is better and more powerful than others. She is petty but also lazy and depending on the scale of the sleight, she is sometimes impressed when people have the courage to even attempt to cause her issue. She is also often lazy and unwanting to deal with anyone who does any small happenstance inconvenience to her. But if you cause her enough of a headache she will stop at nothing to wipe you from the face of history no matter where you go.

Background

Forsythia grew up in the noble circles of Dougstar and Dougleton during the Age of Adventure. The first age after the fall of the empires of scale and flower where the world was a frontier to explore again.

The Mythralight family throughout all of history has been known for its powerful magical bloodline and the perpetual rumour that the first Mythralight was a daughter of The Lady of Arcana herself. As such, Forsythia has trained in and studied magic for all her life and by the time she was an adult was already a highly accomplished wizard.

Though she became a highly accomplished wizard, Forsythia was born into House Mythralight during its lowest point when it was on the brink of collapse. When she was 24, on the 5th of Mystrath, 1,052 FA, her father Eduardo Mythralight died of pnemonia, leaving her as the new head of the house. Historians have since theorised that she may have caused the pnemonia which killed him but there is no clear evidence for this. Her mother still lived and advised her but was ignored until 12 years later when she died in 1,064 FA.

She spent the next three decades of her life rebuilding House Mythralight from the ashes it had nearly fallen to. The first major step toward rebuilding the house was her landing the job as King Robril Starsteel's Arcane Advisor in 1,053 FA. In this single move she gained a considerable amount of power and funds which only grew as she continued acting in her position and developing powerful magic items and spells while she advised the king on all things magical within Dougleton.

On the 6th of Solas, 1,053 FA she would marry Rhodri Roderick Roddenheim who would take her name due to her house's higher status than his.

As she continued in her position, she also went on to expand house Mythralight by having three children. Her firstborn child Viktor Mythralight was born on the 22nd of Owlen 1,055 Fa. She attempted to train him in the arcane arts, but was continually busy with her job as the arcane advisor causing her to spend little time with him in his childhood.

Her second child, Gwendolyn Mythalight would be born on the 27th of Fieldrenary, 1,058 FA. Forsythia put a high degree of care into the conception and birth of Gwendolyn so she would be born in a place of power and on a full moon of Vamperum. Forsythia actually put a great degree of care into Gwendolyn's childhood, attempting to raise her into a powerful wizard like herself, smothering her with incredible amounts of training and studying.

Her third and final child was born on the 5th of Owlen, 1,062 FA. Warren was ignored most of his childhood and eventually sent away to Dawnforge to learn combat skills.

Throughout her time as King Robril Starsteel's Arcane Advisor, she developed over a dozen spells and created hundreds, possibly thousands of magic items. But eventually she rather suddenly packed it all up and left to found Greenest, taking only her husband and children with her as they organised a caravan to settle the area.

Shortly after arrival in Greenest she employed the adventuring party who would later be known as The Saviours of Greenest to gather for her various materials used in the ritual to turn herself and her family into vampires. Gwendolyn Mythalight had not been informed of the ritual and didn't wish to die to complete it, so a confrontation occurred between the two groups, with The Saviours of Greenest successfully saving Gwendolyn from the ritual. The purpose unknown to them, they buried The Mythralights after their suicide to later discover they'd risen from the graves.

Forsythia had another skirmish with the group after kidnapping her daughter back from the group who had saved her. During this time she cut off Gwendolyn's hands to prevent her from casting magic, intending to later turn her into a Vampire alongside the rest of them. The Saviours of Greenest however managed to drive back Forsythia who was forced to retreat due to temporarily losing her magic after turning.
