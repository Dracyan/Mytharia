Rextaulia is based mainly in refinement. They take imports and refine them into other things before exporting them.

Their primarily dealings are in potions and scrolls. Many advanced potioncrafting techniques were pioneered here, or were built on techniques from Dawnforge. Their control of these substances is very tight for fear of the population succumbing to the ill effects of more volatile ingredients or drugs, but due to this and it having so many port cities, black markets and underground trades actually thrive in Rextaulia consequently making it the easiest place to find illicit materials if you're really looking.

Rextaulia is a hybrid country based both over and underground. most major cities have segmants and above and below ground with some smaller towns only being one or the other.

Another thing Rextaulia is known for is, even though their lucent population is rather low, a large amount of them are anomalies, born with different unique abilities and forms of magic that can then often become hereditary
