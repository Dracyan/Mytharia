Vlad is a former member of The Seven Evil Opps, and a villain seeking to turn inflatables into a dominant race in Mytharia. As a young adult he was hit with wild magic that changed him into an inflatable man made of rubber, and it made him feel stronger, it made him feel better than he was before.

He's a man bent on spreading his genes.

For a time, he was a rival to Spider-Volt, until he was swallowed by a dragon and went into hiding. He however, has never stopped his quest for the inflatable race.

Personality

Driven and knowledge hungry, Vlad has spent his life searching for arcane truths. He seeks to understand the magic of the world and find ways to bend it to his goals. Before his transformation, his goals were simply, power and influence, but since his transformation he's developed an obsession with jumpstarting the inflatable race. He carries with him an intense xenophobia of those who are not inflatables like himself because of his perceived superiority over others. He believes himself to be altruistic, that the world will be better if more people are inflated, that people will realise that being inflatable and rubber is superior to being made of flesh and bone.

Background

Vlad was born to the noble house Lanister in Rextaulia. For most of his life, he served the house as a simple arcane researcher. However, one day there was an unprecedented wild magic storm in an area he was researching. While he was researching the storm he was hit with a pure bolt of wild magic that transformed his body. Instead of a yowie, he was now a man made of rubber, an inflatable man. At first he was distraught over the change, but in time, he grew to believe he was better this way, that everyone would be better this way. He had a special rapier enchanted that would cause things to implode or inflate on impact, and donned the mantle of Vlad the Inflator.

In his new identity, Vlad sought to make more inflatables like himself. He began researching spells that changed peoples bodies and ways to do it on people without their consent, when he found out to his own surprise that people did not want the future he wished to create, the bodies he wished to give them. This brought him at odds with Spider-Volt as the two did battle on several occasions. Vlad was defeated several times and his labs destroyed, but he escaped each time and was forced to start again.

This was, until, an auction in the Oktober of 1017 ME organised by Lalythyxion. This auction was to be a major event in the crime world of Renexann with many of the cities most infamous criminals and villains being there. Vlad, as a personal friend of Lalythyxion was of course invited and attended. An adventuring party however, crashed this auction using invitations from Catamari. They sought to steal many of the items up for bid, but the auction was attacked by a dragon of fire. Vlad and one of the adventuers, Terra, tried to fight back against the dragon but both were swallowed in the process. This adventuring party went on to spend several days tracking down the dragons lair so they could rescue their compatriot, and upon slaying the dragon they happened to save Vlad as well. One of these adventurers, Lyre Thair'Monistadt took it upon themself to nurse Vlad back to health in wake of his injuries sustained from the dragon.

Lyre and Vlad grew very close during this time, with their relationship culminating in a one-night stand near the end of Fieldrenary in 1018 ME. They had a brief relationship when Vlad was healed, until Vlad shared his new arcane lab with Lyre and his plan to turn Lyre into an inflatable fox. Lyre immediately was terrified by the situation and feigned support before saying he needed to get milk and leaving Vlad forever. However, Vlad planned for when Lyre got back, to tell them that their one-night stand had ended in him being pregnant. Lyre never returned though and Vlad later found out he was pregnant with triplets. Throughout the pregnancy Vlad debated what he wanted to name the children. He was excited for his inflatable lineage and chose for their future names to be Rory, Douge, and Misty.

When Vlad was a few months away from giving birth, he went to attend his cousin Lexathon's wedding. While he was at a nearby bar the night before the wedding, he met Caspien and realised quickly that he was a gullible kid that he could probably kidnap and turn into an inflatable. So he did just that luring Cas with promise that they would go to the BL bookstore, but on the way, he knocked Cas unconscious and brought him back to his arcane labratory and imprisoned him there to deal with after the wedding was complete. There was however drama with the wedding in which Lexathon told Clarence-Clark that the wedding was going to be called off once everyone got to the venue. So Clarence-Clark planned to arrive late and on his way ran into Lyre Thair'Monistadt who happened to be in town. The pair talked and Lyre offhandedly mentioned his ex that was an inflatable man that was super into eugenics and turning other people into inflatables. Clarence-Clark immediately remembered all the strange comments that Vlad had made about wishing there were more inflatables like him and how he only wishes he could change more people into inflatables like him. Clarence-Clark then snuck into Vlad's house and managed to find his arcane labratory. Inside, he found Cas imprisoned there. The wedding ended up happening with Lexathon being disappointed at Clarence-Clark's absense, and when the wedding was over, Vlad immediately knew he had to get home because he remembered the strange looks he'd gotten from Clarence when discussing his thoughts on turning people into inflatables. Clarence-Clark hears Vlad coming down the stairs and rushes Cas to escape, so Cas summons a meteor to help them escape. The meteor crashes down and Vlad is only partially injured in the explosion. He quickly rushed to get medical attention fearing for the worst, luckily he only had a mild concussion, but unfortunately, he lost the babies.

Relationships

Lyre Thair'Monistadt

Vlad and Lyre had a brief amorous relationship that lead to Vlad becoming pregnant. Vlad still waits for Lyre to return with the milk, so that he can inflate Lyre and they can be together in their true bodies as their best selves. Until he lost the babies, he would continue to send at least weekly sendings to lyre asking when his virile concubine would return. When he lost his and Lyre's babies, he was distraught and blamed himself. Vlad believes he's no longer worthy of Lyre and won't be until he proves himself worthy of that love again, by siring a race of inflatables for him and Lyre to steward together.

Spider-Volt

A rival to Vlad and one who has proved a vexing opponent, Vlad harbours much ire towards The Lightning Spider. Vlad hates him for every time Spider-Volt has destroyed his work and his progress. He sees Spider-Volt as a menace and a constant roadblock to his success and the great world he will create. Vlad believes it is his destiny to spearhead the inflatable race and that Spider-Volt is an insect he will have to crush along the way.
