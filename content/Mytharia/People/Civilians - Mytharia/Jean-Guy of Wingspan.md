Jean-Guy is a wizard of the conjuration guild

### Personality
Jean-Guy puts on an outward front of being timid and shy, but in reality he's arrogant of his spirit binding abilities and thinks he could bind any spirit to his will with just a flick of his wrist. He sees the spirits not as beings to be seen as equals or even to be worshipped, but as servants created for people to control.

### Background
Jean-Guy was born in the city of [[Wingspan]] and spent most of his life there. during his childhood he didn't have much of an affinity for magic but he later attended REMU, focusing on summoning and binding spirits. He wasn't very good at it, nor was he very powerful, but he eventually managed to make a pact with an ink devil who began aiding him in magical training. From there he made a powerful binding vow restricting his spellcasting and magic causing him to become increasingly powerful at the commanding of spirits. He however still masquerades as a low level summoner as to not attract unwanted attention, until he was personally recruited by Flav
