Elafrogos Transformation

Elafrogos is a powerful aberration titan, a radiant moth from beyond the stars who feasts on raw magic to embolden himself and to feed his eternal existence.

Transformation Features

Some specially chosen warlocks of Elafrogos gain the following features

Prerequisites

Ability Scores: Charisma 13
Roleplay: You must be attuned with Elafrogos and have a close enough bond with him that he grants you this transformation.
Transformation Save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier

Level Milestones

The following are examples of possible level milestones for Elafrogos’ chosen:

  • Defeating a powerful magical being and eating it
  • Doing a deed that receives monumental praise from Elafrogos
  • Sacrificing a large piece of yourself to Elafrogos
  • Being hit by a wave of powerful magic
  • Consuming another being undergoing this transformation

Elafrogos Stage 1

Starting at 1st level, you gain the following Transformation Boons and this level’s Transformation Flaw.

Stage 1 Boon: Sunk Life Fallacy

Gus brands you as one of his favoured warlocks and makes a deal with you. You can sacrifice percentages of your life to cast additional spells or upcast spells if you run out of spell slots. At the DM’s discretion they may also allow you to use it to regain other features or magic item charges.

Stage 1 Flaw: Arcane Hunger

A fragment of Gus’ hunger inhabits your body. The magic that sustains yourself slowly wanes and you must consume more every few days to maintain your soul and your life. This counter starts at 5 and decreases by 1 everyday. To add to this counter you must consume magic. Whether that be the flesh of a magical creature, a magic item, or some other form of mana. If you do not consume enough magic your body gives out and you die from the lack of energy.

Elafrogos Stage 2

When you reach Stage 2, you select one appropriate Stage 2 Boon and also gain the Stage 2 flaws

Stage 2: Boon: Starry Wings

You sprout moth-like wings filled with glowing stars and eyes. When unfurled, these wings emanate bright like in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet after that. Additionally, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed. If you already had a flying speed, your existing wings if you have them become covered in stars and eyes as per above and your flying speed increases by 20 feet.

Stage 2 Boon: Elanthian Mind

Elafrogos augments your mind with his caterpillar protecting you from outside influence. You gain resistance to psychic damage, and advantage on all wisdom saving throws that would affect your free will in any way. Additionally, you are immune to having your memories modified by anyone other than Elafrogos.

Stage 2 Boon: Glyph Antlers

Antlers sprout from your head, sharp horns embedded with glyphs. If you already had horns or antlers, they become more jagged and twisting and the glyphs burn themselves into them. Choose two spells from the wizard spell list of levels 1-3, you learn these spells and gain the ability to cast them once a day if you could not already cast spells. As a reaction when you roll initiative you can choose to cast a spell that targets either you or an ally within range.

Stage 2 Flaw: Mothification

Fur and hair grows from your skin and your eyes balloon to huge proportions making you take the visage of a moth-like variant of yourself. You become attracted to light wanting to swallow and eat sources of light especially those magical. 

Elafrogos Stage 3

When you reach Stage 3 of your Elafrogos transformation, you select one appropriate Stage 3 Boon, and also gain the Stage 3 Flaw

Stage 3 Boon: Otherworldly Psionics

Elafrogos further augments your mind and empowers you with the psionic energy to cast the following spells:
Detect thoughts, mass suggestion, intrusive thought, dominate person, and modify memory.
You may cast from this list of spells three times per day and regain expended uses on a long rest. Additionally, if a creature would have advantage on the saving throw against any of these spells, they instead roll with disadvantage as Elafrogos bends the defences of their own mind against them.

Stage 3 Boon: Interstellar Luminance

Prerequisite: Starry Wings
Your body toughens and becomes impervious to pressure. You gain the ability to hold your breath for up to a day and your body becomes able to withstand the pressure of space and the depths of the ocean.
Additionally, you gain the ability to cause your wings to glow brightly with interstellar luminance. You gain the ability to cast the blinding radiance spell without expending spell slots, instead reducing the number of days you have until you must consume more magic by 1.

Stage 3 Boon: Magical Regurgitation

You gain the ability to more selectively refine and process the magical corpses you consume. Within 24 hours of consuming a magical corpse, you can spend 1 minute to regurgitate a single magic component from the corpse. This component must have a harvesting DC less than 25 and cannot be an essence.

Stage 3 Flaw: Death’s Head

Your mouth transforms into a horrifying cavern of teeth with sectioned parts that can fold open to reveal it as well as a proboscis for liquid foods. Your appetite becomes harder to control and you now must consume magic every 3 days instead of five and the magic you must consume must be stronger. You gain a bite attack that deals 6d4 piercing damage and when you are hungry the urge to eat light and magic sources can be overwhelming. Your DM may call upon you to make a DC 16 wisdom saving throw, (ignoring any benefits gained from this transformation) or be overcome with the urge to consume.

Elafrogos Stage 4

Stage 4 Boon: Void Jumper

Prerequisite: Interstellar Luminance
You gain the power of a titan to fold space. Your flying speed increases by 30 feet and you gain the ability to cast plane shift once per day. When you cast plane shift in this way you can jump across space or to other realities connected to your own. Additionally, your blood becomes that of minor titan blood, and you count as a titan for the purposes of spells or effects against them.

Stage 4 Boon: Ravenous Adaptations

You grow a massive mouth along the centre of your torso full of jagged teeth. With this mouth you can more easily consume the corpses of magical creatures. Once per day when you consume a magical creature in this way you can choose one ability from the creature’s statblock and gain it as if it was an ability of your own. Your body changes to accommodate the feature and it persists for 24 hours or until you take a long rest.

Stage 4 Boon: Arcane Consumption

Prerequisite: Can cast spells
You grow a massive mouth along the centre of your torso full of jagged teeth. With this mouth you can consume magic directly and funnel it straight into your soul. You can consume spell scrolls from any class. When you consume a spell scroll from another class, you learn that spell as if it were a known spell for you, however you can only prepare an amount of these spells per day equal to your spellcasting ability modifier. Additionally, you can only prepare a spell consumed in this way if you possess the spell slots to cast it.

Stage 4 Flaw: Undying Feast

You grow one size category larger than you already were, the DC to avoid giving in to urges of hunger becomes 20, and your creature type becomes aberration for the purposes of effects and spells against them. Additionally, the magic you must consume every few days grows in strength. You must consume magic equivalent to 7th level each time you wish to replenish your body, and you must do so each day to survive.
