Description

As an action, you can draw a card from the deck. You then immediately become affected by that card and associated effect. You can only draw from this deck five times per day, resetting each day at dawn. This limit is not in effect when playing this decks associated game.

A table of the associated cards and effects is listed below.

Roll Number Card Effect Description
1 0 The Fool Spawns a random creature determined by a roll on the random creature table.
2 1 The Magician You receive a scroll of one random spell.
3 2 The High Priestess You gain the priestess card. At any time as a bonus action you can select a creature within range and cause otherworldly hands to attempt to pull that creature out of existence, forcing them to make a DC 7 charisma saving throw. On a failure, the creature is lifted to another plane of existence. If the creature fails by 5 or more they are pulled from all of existence as a whole. On a success they resist the effect and the hands unhand them. Once you receive the card you must use it within 1 day. Once the card has been used, the hands take it from you.
4 3 The Empress Half of your hit points are restored
5 4 The Emperor Half your spell slots for each level rounded down are restored. If you would round to 0 roll a d4, on a 4 you recharge the slot. You gain 1 level of exhaustion.
6 5 Hierophant You may ask one question to INSERT ENTITY, to which it must answer truthfully
7 6 The Lovers You become immediately married to the nearest creature to you.
8 7 The Chariot The heroes feast spell is cast centred on you. The entire meal is themed around crabs.
9 8 Strength The next time you hit an attack in the next 24 hours, you land a critical hit and may roll for a double crit as normal.
10 9 The Hermit An amount of gold rains above you equal to xd20, x being twice your level
11 10 The Wheel of Fortune Draw 5 random cards from the top of the deck and all effects take place.
12 11 Justice You gain a lawyer who will defend you in court for any crimes you commit.
13 12 The Hanged Man You flip upside down but can move normally sliding along the ground as if you were walking while moving your legs in a walking motion. This effect ends after 24 hours have passed.

17 16 The Tower You recieve a random bomb, determined by the random bombs table.
18 17 The Star Your movement speed is doubled for the next 24 hours. When you run, stars spring out from your steps. You gain the ability to attempt to run through someone as an action. A creature may elect to make a Dexterity or Strength saving throw. A dexterity saving throw if attempting to dodge, or a strength saving throw if attempting to withstand it. The DC equals 8 + your constitution modifier + your proficiency bonus. On a failed save, a creature takes bludgeoning damage equal to your hit dice. If this would reduce the creature to 0 hit points, you run directly through them as they are turned into a fine mist.

23 E1 Osiris Apples rain from above.
24 E2 Horus You heal an amount of health equal to half of your hit dice + your constitution modifier on each roll. In addition, you may choose one disease or condition with which you are afflicted and it burns away, leaving you healed.
25 E3 Geb You Become Afflicted with the Wild Plague

29 E7 Tohth For the next 24 hours you can speak with the being who created the deck only to gain information.
30 E8 Anubis You may choose one dead creature you know and roll an immediate resurrection check on them without modifiers. If the reincarnation is successful, the creature grows their new body out of a nearby plant.
31 E9 Atum The next time you reduce a creature to 0 hit points, you steal that creature's soul. You can use this soul as an essence in enchanting, or for whatever purpose you decide.
