Vulpalf's are a race of people from a starfaring empire at war with the Begacahkos and Saiyans. They are a proud people focused on the creation of better and better technology and cataloguing the lore of the universe as they force way for their own stories.

### Appearance
Vulpalf are bipedal humanoids with mostly humanoid proportions. They possess tails and ears remininscent of foxes as well as fur along their arms and legs. They also possess fox-like noses and the beginnings of snouts with sharp teeth. Vulpalf bodies are typically physically strong allowing for the ability to life heavy weights. They posess a breath sac within their throats which allows them to channel cold and emit a breath of frost that removes energy from their surroundings. They also possess the ability to channel this through their veins and out small organic vents on the undersides of their wrists.

Vulpalfs are also gifted with powerful energy that strengthens their minds making it much easier for them to maintain focus and to resist psychic influence and attacks.
