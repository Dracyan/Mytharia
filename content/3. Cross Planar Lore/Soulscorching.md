Soulscorching is the process by which a creature died by overexerting their soul or the magic therein. Upon a creature's death in this way, they typically rise as a malevolent spirit called a BLANK. This is paricularly common among Illuminants whose primary cause of death is via the overexertion of their magic burning out their body.

These spirits become malformed representations of the creature they once were using the magic from their body. This in turn makes the Soulscorched Spirits of Illuminants particularly deadly.

Once someone has been transformed into a soulscorched spirit they cannot be cured except by a wish spell, and they cannot be resurrected except by the wish spell.
