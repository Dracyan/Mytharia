Pharos' are a rare form of lucent beings who possess the capability to manifest and exteralise their soul as a spirit that represents them as their prime concept. These spirits can possess a myriad of abilities depending on those who manifest them.
### Description
Pharos' are the counterpart to [[Lodestar]]s. Where a lodestar externalises their soul via the crystalisation of it and forging it into an emblem, a Pharos externalises it through manifesting it as a spirit called a shadow bitch with abilities that represent the personality and goals of the individual.

### Abilities
A pharos' shadow bitch may have useful abilities, such as splitting the energy of a spell between the pharos and another, or making the heads of all those who gaze upon it explode. But they could also have useless abilities like being able to topdeck the card you need to win a card game, or changing the flavour of water into wine.

The abilities of pharos' potential shadow bitches are wildly unpredictable and could be anything one could imagine. These abilities can also change and grow with the person who controls them as they get older. A shadow bitch might be useless when it first manifests and then gain further abilities as the pharos becomes more powerful and finds who they are as an individual.
