Arthur Swifteye was a bandit from town of Harisonarkansas. He grew up in a loving adopted family who took him in after he was abandoned for being a witch in Rascatine . Some believe he was related to the former royal family of Rascatine but the validity of this is unknown.

Arthur is credited as being one of The Saviours of Greenest, and one of the heroes who saved all of Mytharia from being torn apart in The Planaclysm , however in stopping the planaclysm he perished at the hands of Avanthum.

Personality

Arthur Swifteye was a bandit. He didn't care about laws but he cared about people. He cared about his friends and about saving the world, especially if there was coin involved. His main focus was always his own life and to keep driving forward in the world. Arthur believed that no matter who you lose, carrying on is the most important things. In the end however, his way of thinking must have changed as he did sacrifice himself to end The Planaclysm and kill Avanthum after achieving Apotheosis.

History

Arthur was born in the country of Rascatine to unknown parents. Some believe they were nobles in Rascine city but this has never been confirmed.

He was abandoned in the desert for being born a witch but was found and adopted by a family in Harrisonarkansas who took him in as their own along with their own son. He had to hide inside most days and eventually found family among a band of outlaws on the run, and specifically in his friend Dutch. They would go on to become wanted outlaws together prowling the deserts of Rascatine robbing banks and the established government.

One day they went on a heist to steal from a museum. Arthur and Dutch each grabbed something before the guards came and chased them out of the building. They ran and ran and ran, and eventually one of the guards got Dutch with a spear as he fell to the ground. Arthur assumed him dead and kept running. Now wanted with an even higher bounty he hid out on a boat and fled to Vortil landing in Goldas before making his way to Dougstar . Here he would do odd jobs before eventually getting hired as a caravan guard by the Mythralights on their way to establish Greenest .

On the way to Greenest he met Chet Alexopolus, Primula Exspiravit , and Rin . and they defended the caravan together until it made its way to the site in which Greenest would be established.

Within the first week of Greenest's establishment there was a devastating werewolf attack where most of its would-be citizens died. But Arthur and his allies perservered as did the Mythralights and several others such as Ghag .

After this the party began looking for ways to defend themselves and the town for when the next full moon arrived. This began with delving into a Remnant ruin. They found multiple useful items, but were attacked by robotic birds which killed Arthur. After his death Rin burned his body in a pile of potatoes with a prayer to The Muffin King to resurrect him. It did not work but Forsythia Mythralight took notice of this and had him resurrected to aid her in plans she was undertaking.

Arthur along with his party began doing several missions for Forsythia, one of them supposedly involving a Troll . Another saw him and a party member alledgedly having met the goddess Twilow, The Hearth Mother and lying successfully lying to her.

Once they had gathered what was needed it is unknown what exactly ocurred that night other than a mass suicide of some kind.

Arthur and his associates did more adventuring for some time until one day bounty hunters began coming after Arthur. One of these hunters being Xomus Sunbreath, a former associate of Arthur during his outlaw days in Rascatine. Xomus let slip that Dutch was alive before Arthur put a bullet in their head. From there the group went for a boat immediatly and sailed out to Rascatine. Arthur managed to save Dutch who had lost an arm and a leg, but ironically Arthur lost an arm himself in his battle with the king.

Upon their return to Vortil, The Planaclysm began as the Dragons arrived to invade Mytharia led by Avanthum .

Arthur and his gang defended Greenest from a green dragon whom they killed and a blue dragon that they scarred and scared away. After this Arthur recieved a letter from Chet asking for aid in defending Molbrut . They did not give Chet aid and he fell with Molbrut. There were bigger fish to fry as Plavoth was sundered from Vortil and Avanthum landed and scorched Plavoth along with the energy of The Blazenfields.

A final confrontation occurred between Arthur and his allies against Avanthum. It is believed that Arthur achieved Apotheosis during this battle but died in killing Avanthum.
