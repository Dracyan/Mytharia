Changelings are a titanborn race of humanoids from one of Mytharia's moons, Nokk. Their ancestry, and the magical radiation present on Nokk have caused their physiology to be malleable allowing them to shapeshift. All changelings are born with near the exact same appearance, but possess the ability to change it and a large amount of their physiology at will.

Appearance

A changelings natural form is an androgynous bipedal humanoid with pale white skin. They have two arms and are bald. In their natural form, changelings do not possess genitals. Most changelings are born lucent with pointed ears in this form, but even those born aphotic can use their transformation abilities to appear as if they have pointed ears or vice versa. A changelings natural form doesn't matter however, as each changeling creates their own form(s) over the course of their lifetime.

At will, a changeling can manipulate their body changing parts of its shape, its colour, giving themselves hair, or changing parts of their anatomy. Everything except the basic structure of their body, IE, the amount of limbs, essential organs, and whether they have blood or not can be changed cosmetically.

Genetics and Reproduction

Reproduction is handled primarily in one of two ways. As changelings are a titanborn race, either their titan births more, or changelings give themselves genitalia and reproduce sexually. Other alternatives are possible but less common. Wild magic has been known on occasion to manifest changelings, or transform people and objects into them.

Changelings are known practitioners of eugenics. On occasion changelings will be born with defects of some kind, extra limbs, a serpents tail instead of legs, etc. If the Lunar Conclave can find a use for changelings with these defects, they are allowed a place in society, if not, they are exiled, sent to be experimented on, or killed. Other than that, the place of genetics are not of much consequence to changelings. They all appear nearly the same, and when a child is born its genetics are seemingly randomised. Changelings still hold the cultural ideals of families and those they are raised by and related to, but genetics don't seem to be something that is passed on.

Another benefit of changelings transformation abilities, is that they can manipulate their bone marrow and change their blood type at will. This often makes them sought after in medical fields outside of Nokk, and in adventuring parties with a lot of racial disparity because they are universal blood donors.

At times, changelings will use magic to lock their form and live among other races as one of them. during that time their physiology will continually adapt to try and become more and more like the members of that race around them. This can sometimes allow a changeling to eventually figure out how to adapt more difficult parts of a creatures anatomy. Most of the time, a changeling cannot adapt a jade troll's regenerative abilities, or a siren's song, but if they spend enough time in a single form and among others of the race they're mimicking, they can eventually learn to give themselves these pieces of anatomy. This can even in cases allow a changeling to adapt their lifespan to the lifespan of the other race. Typically transformation lock is only used on replacement children however.

History

Changelings came into being before The Shattering and when it occurred were shunted into The Feywild and onto The Feywild's version of the moon of Nokk. There, their civilisation flourished, with their largest cities being lead each primarily by one of the titans that birthed them. The high abundance of mana on the fae moon allowed them to build a highly advanced civilisation based on magic.

Eventually, the changeling society found that its knowledge of the world had plateaued, and they needed to find out more. So they used the power of the titans that spawned them to pierce the veil between worlds, and devised a process to lock newborn changelings into a single form and replace the children of other races, in other worlds.

As time went on, changelings would send out more and more spies, and find ways to encode memories in the changeling to unlock when they came of age. This allowed them to immerse themselves and grow up in a culture, before learning about their changeling heritage. When a changeling sleeper agent came of age, the encoded memories would awaken and tell them what they are, who their birth parents were and their jobs, what their birth name was, a small amount of information on where they were born, and what their mission was for why they were sent to replace a child somewhere. The details of these missions varied, they could be as simple as learning about the culture and reporting back, or as complicated as destabilising the nation and pursuing specific political ends if they had replaced a future king. The lives of the taken children varied. in some cases they would be raised by the changeling parents as a form of perceived cultural exchange. In some cases, the children may also be taken to perform experiments on, to learn about the limits and capabilities of their physiology.

The lives of these changelings adopted into other races could vary. Many changelings would go their entire lives undetected. In many cases their quality of life was poor due to not having the physiological abilities of these races, such as a dragonborn's breath sac, or the ability to trance like an elf. And of course in some cases even if their forms didn't look like they were lucent, changelings were burned upon presenting other signs of being lucent in anti-magic societies.

When The Planaclysm occurred, changelings were shunted onto Mytharia's moon of Nokk. Many of their cities were destroyed including massive pieces of their archives. Much of their knowledge was lost and much of their political landscape was turned over and destabilised with different powers taking control of their society. The replacement of children in Mytharia became more common as the Lunar Conclave, the ruling council of changeling society, sought to replace the knowledge they had lost and to shape the world how they wished.

Cultural Ideals

In changeling society, they are born without gender and usually choose it for themselves as they age and find their preferences. Genderfluidity is also common in changeling culture, choosing to adopt different genders or forms based on circumstance, like night and day, or months of the year, or simply on a whim.

Theorised Origin

Changelings are primarily believed to have come into being originally from a species of plant titan that bore them as fruit. Some believe changelings were manifested from the magic of Nokk itself bringing them into existence.
