Andy 'Zuliani' Zuliani is a waiter at the Two Heels Diner in Zappingham. He was originally born in Fulmin and grew up there before moving around to earn his degrees. He did a bachelors in fashion in Fulmin before doing a masters degree in shoe design in Sparkopolis. However Andy has struggled to find a career with his degrees and has ended up working at as a waiter.

Personality

Andy is a pretty laid back guy who just wants to get through his day to day at the diner so he can go home to further his personal work. He's driven to succeed in his creative work but is continually finding it hard to continue and find opportunities for these endeavours under the reign of The Volt King.

Background

In 1970 AVK, Andy was born to an average suburban family in Fulmin and grew up there throughout most of his early life. During his teenage years he became exposed to the world of fashion and particularly the intricacies of heels and the culture around them. This sparked a lifelong passion as when he graduated high school he moved on to attend Sparkfire University to complete a bachelors in fashion design.

During his years at Sparkfire University is also when he realised the art world chafes a lot under the rule of the volt king and decided to take burger flipping and restaurant waitering as electives so he'd have skills to fall back on.

After his stint at Sparkfire University, Andy went on to do a masters degree in shoe design in Sparkopolis, minoring in fashion politics. It was during this time that Andy became active in the underground art world of protest and propaganda against The Volt King. He attended many secret events and protests, as well as a lot of parties while he was in the midst of his degree.

However, once his masters was complete Andy became loosely involved with an organisation known as The Cloudbreakers. He designed the shoes for their outfits and composed their theme song. But when most of The Cloudbreakers were killed by the wrath of The Volt King and the efforts of Volt Action during the failed Operation Cloudfall, he decided that for now he just wants to get through the day and hung up shoemaking falling back onto becoming waiter and getting a job at a small diner outside Sparkopolis called the Wayfarer's Lighthouse.

Andy was working at the diner one evening when Two Wordz came in shortly after meeting Wyenne Belswanna Coochiggly Murdendrum, rescuing her from her abusive stepmother, and fighting a kraken. The band was being served by Andy with Goojoo even ordering his favourite, the Ruben, when the diner was attacked by a jellyfish-like titan. This titan had been there for Wyenne's locket which the group was going to give to them until one of their members, Trevor, leapt through a portal the titan created last minute to make sure it didn't steal the locket.

After this terrifying attack on the diner, he quickly quit his job and decided it was time to move. He moved continents, going so far as to move all the way to Zappingham where he got a job at the Two Heels Diner.

His uneventful work was however interrupted a few months later when The Outsiders met Two Wordz at this diner and were attacked by Viles Vorals of Volt Action for their crimes against the fulminated crown. As this battle occurred instead of hiding, Andy ran to get out of there as soon as possible.
