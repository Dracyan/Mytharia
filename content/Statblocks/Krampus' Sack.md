Wondrous item, legendary
This large sac is made of a thick black fur with several small patches sewn in in different places.

When you have a creature grappled, as an action you can place them inside the bag, where they remain as long as they remain grappled. The bag has AC 17 and 20 Hit Points and can damaged by slashing weapons but has resistance to piercing and is immune to bludgeoning. The weight of creatures in the bag doesn’t count against your carrying capacity. Up to four Small creatures or two Medium creatures can fit in the bag at a time.

If a creature begins its turn inside this bag, they must make a DC 21 Charisma saving throw. On a failed saving throw, the creature regresses into the form of a child for 24 hours. The creature becomes one size smaller and has disadvantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws. The creature’s equipment does not reduce in size. This is considered a curse that requires a ground of goats hoof to cure.
